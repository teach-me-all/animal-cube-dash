Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Animal Cube Dash does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is important to us.

Overview

Animal Cube Dash ("the App") is a watchOS game developed by Ahaana Sistla. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers or usage analytics
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect health or fitness data

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores game progress data locally on your Apple Watch using UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Current level and highest level reached
  • Selected skin and unlocked skins
  • Best completion times
  • Total levels completed and total deaths
  • Consecutive perfect level count

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party. You can reset all game data at any time from within the App.

Game Center

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and friend features. Game Center is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. When you use Game Center features:

  • Your Game Center display name may be visible to other players on leaderboards
  • Score submissions are handled by Apple's Game Center service
  • Friend interactions are managed through Apple's Game Center infrastructure

Game Center authentication and data handling are entirely managed by Apple. We do not have access to your Game Center account information.

Messages Integration

The App allows you to share brag messages via the Messages app. When you choose to share via Messages:

  • The App opens the Messages app using the system URL scheme
  • We do not have access to your contacts or message content
  • Message sending is handled entirely by the Messages app

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. There are no ads in the App.

Children's Privacy

Animal Cube Dash is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for children of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

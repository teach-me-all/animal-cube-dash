Frequently Asked Questions

How do I jump?

Tap anywhere on the screen to make your animal cube jump. Time your jumps to avoid obstacles and cross gaps between platforms.

How do I duck under flying knives?

Swipe down on the screen to duck. Your animal cube will crouch for a short time, allowing flying knives to pass overhead. The cube automatically stands back up after a moment.

How do I unlock new skins?

Skins unlock as you progress through levels. Common skins require completing 10 levels. Rare, Ultra Rare, and Epic skins require finishing a level with 2 or more lives remaining. Legendary skins require passing 5 consecutive levels without losing any lives.

How do I change my animal cube skin?

Go to the Skins gallery from the home screen. Tap on any unlocked skin, then press "Select" to use it. You can also press "Brag" to share your unlock with friends.

How does Game Center work?

Game Center lets you compete on leaderboards and connect with friends. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center on your iPhone (Settings → Game Center). Your Apple Watch will use your iPhone's Game Center account.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes, you can reset all game progress from within the app. This will reset your level, unlocked skins, and all statistics. Note that this action cannot be undone.

What Apple Watch models are supported?

Animal Cube Dash requires watchOS 10.0 or later. This includes Apple Watch Series 6 and newer, Apple Watch SE (1st and 2nd generation), and Apple Watch Ultra models.

Does the app use any data or require an internet connection?

No. Animal Cube Dash works entirely offline. All game data is stored locally on your Apple Watch. An internet connection is only needed for optional Game Center features like leaderboards.
